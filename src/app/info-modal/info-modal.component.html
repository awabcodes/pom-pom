Info Step 1

Create and Choose a task you'd like to get done

 Step 2

Start the Pomodoro Timer

 Step 3

Work on the task until the Pomodoro rings

 Step 4

Take a short break After Each Pomodoro (5 Minutes)

 Step 5

Every 4 pomodoros, take a longer break (20 - 30 Minutes)

 Benifits of The Pomodoro

1. Find out how much effort an activity requires.

2. Cut down on interruptions.

3. Estimate the effort for activities.
